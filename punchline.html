Punchline!

As a first experience in game development, me and a group of student collegues and friends decided to participate to the annual Ludum Dare competition.
Each Ludum Dare usually gives developers three days to complete a game, while also specifying a certain theme. For the 53rd competition, it was delivery.
Knowing that most other devs would make something centered around the physical act of delivery, we decided to take a more abstract approach.

In Punchline! you help an amatuer comedian deliver his best joke by playing a series of minigames that are happening in his head.

During development, these were my responsibilities:

  • Shading and VFX: I created all VFX and the Shaders used in the game using Unity's Shader Graph and Particle System.
  • 3D Modeling: All 3D assets were created and textured in Blender.
  • Gameplay Programming: While all of the members of the group handled programming, i particularly focused on the gameflow for the last minigame.
  • Game Design: Every member of the team participated in designing aspects of the game, from theme to gameplay ideas.
