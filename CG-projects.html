Farming Simulator Map Converter for Unity

This project was part of a 6 month internship at CNH Industrial and involved developing a Unity3D tool that could automate the process of importing/texturing Farming Simulator maps into a HDRP Unity project.
These scenarios are then used in a Dynamic Simulator that allows a person to drive around the environment inside a virtual tractor.

Procedural Solar System

This WebGL app was developed as part of my first Computer Graphics course at the University of Bologna.
It uses Perlin noise to texturize the planets of a small solar system.
The player can move around the environment with a simple starship model.
